    Covid 19 has caused world wide shutdowns and has been the first global pandemic in a long time. Currently Coronavirus world wide is expected to continue to plague us with no end date in sight. Current vaccines are expected to be completed at a minimum 12 to 18 months. And even then it takes time to produce and spread. I said all this to say that a cure isn't coming soon so the best methods are prevention methods

    For those who may not be informed and for reconfimation. Covid 19 is a novel coronavirs which has taken the lives of over 250 thousand lives world wide and spreads primarily from person to person through small droplets from the nose or mouth, which are expelled when a person with COVID-19 coughs, sneezes, or speaks. People can catch COVID-19 if they breathe in these droplets from a person infected with the virus. These droplets can land on objects and surfaces around the person such as tables, doorknobs and handrails. People can become infected by touching these objects or surfaces, then touching their eyes, nose or mouth.

    A novel coronavirus is a new strain of coronavirus that has not been previously identified in humans. Coronaviruses (CoV) are a large family of viruses transmitting between animals and people that cause illness ranging from the common cold to more severe diseases such as Middle East respiratory syndrome (MERS-CoV) and severe acute respiratory syndrome (SARS-CoV).
